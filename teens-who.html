TeensWHO is a division of E. M. Phillips Consulting dedicated to helping teen girls fulfill their academic and life goals worldwide.

We provide scholarships and grants to teen girls entering college or undertaking a course. Training, development, and support are offered case-by-case to teen movers and shakers worldwide.

If you're a Teenpreneur and want to start a new venture or seek assistance to develop or expand your brand, we may be able to help!
